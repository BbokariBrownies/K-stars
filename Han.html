Han

Han is the 1st member of SKZ to start the Maknae Line (Youngest). He is 23 years old. He is the main rapper,vocalist and producer of SKZ. His full name is Han Ji-Sung. He is part of the subset within the group called 3Racha. His stage name in 3racha is J.ONE. He has the title of 4th Gen ACE or All-rounder, meaning he is exceptional in every skill like dancing, rapping and vocals in all of 4th generation Kpop.
