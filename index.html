All about SKZ

Skz is a Korean music band was formed in 2018 through a reality show. They are under the JYP entertainment company. They are known as the leaders of the next generation of Korean Music and are Mega famous in many parts of the world. It consists of eight members; Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin,Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin and I.N. Their songs have been streamed 1.8 billion times and their views are totalling up to 5 billion.
